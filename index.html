Hello, {this.props.name}

Unmounting component

  • componentWillUnmount()

This method is invoked immediately before a component is unmounted and destroyed

Virtual DOM

  • Virtual DOM is a copy of a real DOM
  • Updating the small parts of a web app without affecting other parts of the UI
  • Updates are performed very quickly
